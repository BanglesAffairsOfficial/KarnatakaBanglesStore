## ⚠️ Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Risks

#### 1. **Competition Risk** 🎯 HIGH
- **Threat:** Shopify, WooCommerce entering hybrid market
- **Mitigation:**
  - Focus on niches (India, SMBs, digital products)
  - Build community moat
  - Maintain feature velocity
  - Stay cost-competitive

#### 2. **Product-Market Fit Risk** 🎯 MEDIUM
- **Threat:** Market doesn't want hybrid solution
- **Mitigation:**
  - User interviews (20+/month)
  - Beta program (500+ users)
  - Early NPS tracking
  - Pivot capability

#### 3. **Churn Risk** 🎯 MEDIUM
- **Threat:** Customers leave after 3 months
- **Mitigation:**
  - Strong onboarding
  - Proactive customer success
  - Feature improvements based on feedback
  - Monthly engagement campaigns

#### 4. **Scaling Risk** 🎯 MEDIUM
- **Threat:** Infrastructure can't handle growth
- **Mitigation:**
  - Auto-scaling from day 1
  - Load testing monthly
  - Database optimization
  - CDN caching strategy

#### 5. **Payment Gateway Risk** 🎯 LOW
- **Threat:** Razorpay account suspension
- **Mitigation:**
  - Multiple gateway integration
  - Proper compliance
  - Fraud detection system
  - Quick action on issues
